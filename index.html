Europe's Michelin Star Restaurants

Europe has the most Michelin Stars our of all continents, and in this article we will explore on how awards are ditributed amongst country and cuisine. We will also explore how price is distributed, and which country has the cheapest and most expensive average price.

* Try zooming and dragging the map using mouse

France has the most award received out of all country in Europe, followed by Italy and Germany. In term of cuisine, most stars are awarded to Modern Cuisine and Creative.

Price plays an important role in deciding the restaurant for some people. Belgium has the lowest average minimum price (cheapest course), and Denmark has the highest average minimum and maximum price. In term of price distribution, higher star restaurants are more expensive in average.
